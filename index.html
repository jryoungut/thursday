How To

Fill out this report every Thursday morning. By signing (electronically with your full name) and clicking the Submit button you verify that you have thoroughly inspected the selected apparatus.

Logistics will review and act upon each repair needed listed in the report in a timely manner.

For emergency repairs call Eric Staten (801-598-7978), or after-hours and holidays call the on-call logistics person.
